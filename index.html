About Pasta

Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from wheat flour and water. It comes in many shapes like spaghetti, penne, and fusilli.
Pasta is popular all over the world and can be served with sauces like tomato, Alfredo, or pesto. Some types of pasta are filled, like ravioli, and others are baked, like lasagna.
It can be eaten hot or cold and is often enjoyed with cheese, vegetables, or meat.
