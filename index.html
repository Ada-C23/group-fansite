Meet the Capybara: World’s Largest and Friendliest Rodent

Capybaras are the world’s largest rodents and some of the friendliest animals on Earth. Native to South America, these gentle giants can weigh up to 150 pounds as adults. They are highly social and thrive in groups, often relaxing together near water. Built for a semi-aquatic life, capybaras have webbed feet and love to swim—spending hours in rivers and ponds to stay cool. Their calm nature and tolerance make them easygoing companions to many other animals. With their peaceful demeanor and unique charm, capybaras have captured the hearts of animal lovers around the world.

Featured Fun Fact: Capybaras are Nature’s Ottoman

Capybaras are lovingly known as “Nature’s Ottoman” because of their calm nature and surprising talent for being a comfy seat. Birds, monkeys, and other animals love lounging on them—capybaras don’t seem to mind! Their laid-back vibe and friendliness make them the ultimate chill companions in the animal kingdom. It’s just one more reason these gentle giants have won our hearts.
