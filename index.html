Meet the Code & Cake Ladies

Once upon a semicolon, four legendary ladies — Lina, Olya, Qiaoqiao and Kate — found themselves at the intersection of sweet treatsband clean code.

By daylight, they squashed bugs and crafted beautiful functions. By night, they debated buttercream vs ganache while sipping hot cocoa and reviewing pull requests.

Lina couldn’t resist a slice of lemon tart after a long session of debugging.

Olya preferred vanilla pound cake with unit tests.

Qiaoqiao was all about crème brulée and semantic HTML.

Kate was the queen of cheesecake and CSS animations.

Together, they created not just web pages, but edible elegance. Some say if you view the source of this site, you can still smell the cake crumbs and see their perfectly nested tags.
