A glimpse into the Evyy Cafe experience!

Evyy Café was founded in 2025 by four passionate friends: Ellie, Viktoriya, Yasiel, and Yekaterina, whose initials inspired the café's name. What began as casual baking sessions in Ellie's kitchen blossomed into a shared dream of creating a space where their love for global desserts could be shared with the community. Each founder brings their unique cultural heritage to the menu, resulting in a delightful fusion of flavors from around the world. Today, Evyy Café stands as a testament to friendship, creativity, and the universal language of sweetness that brings people together.
