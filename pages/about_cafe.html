About Creators of Evyy

Evyy Café is the heartwarming creation of four passionate friends: Ellie, Victoria, Yasiel, and Yekaterina. Each brings a unique soul to the space—Yasiel, the dreamer with a flair for vintage aesthetics; Ellie, the storyteller whose warmth shapes every cozy corner; Yekaterina, a culinary artist inspired by her Eastern European roots; and Victoria, the strategist with a love for community and culture. Together, they’ve crafted more than just a retro café—they’ve created a sanctuary where nostalgia, flavor, and friendship meet.
