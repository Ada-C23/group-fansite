Our Favorite Dessert Recipes

Welcome to Evyy Cafe's most cherished dessert recipes! These delightful treats are customer favorites at our cozy cafe, and now you can recreate the magic in your own kitchen. Each recipe has been carefully selected by our team – Ellie, Viktoriya, Yasiel, and Yekaterina – to bring a taste of our retro cafe experience to your home. Enjoy these global desserts that tell stories as rich as their flavors!

Devil's Food Cake

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 10-12

This American classic is one of Ellie's specialties at Evyy Cafe. Our customers adore the deep chocolate flavor and impossibly moist texture. We often serve it with a scoop of homemade vanilla bean ice cream for a perfect contrast of flavors.

Tres Leches

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Chilling Time: 4 hours (or overnight)

Servings: 9-12

Yasiel brought this Latin American treasure to our menu, and it quickly became a weekend special that sells out by noon. The three-milk soak creates a dessert that's rich yet light, making it the perfect companion to our house-blend coffee.

Woodpile Cake / Crepe Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes (for all crepes)

Chilling Time: 2 hours

Servings: 8-10

Yekaterina's Moldovan heritage shines through in this beautiful layered dessert. Drawing from her childhood memories in Moldova, she introduced this traditional crepe cake to our menu, where its delicate layers and subtle flavors have made it a customer favorite for special occasions.

Napoleon Cake

Preparation Time: 1 hour

Baking Time: 30 minutes

Chilling Time: 6 hours (or overnight)

Servings: 10-12

Viktoriia, who comes from Central Asia, introduced us to this Russian classic. The Napoleon cake has become our signature dessert, with its impressive layers of flaky pastry and smooth custard creating a textural masterpiece. Viktoriia's version has a unique spice blend that reflects her Central Asian roots while honoring the traditional Russian recipe.
