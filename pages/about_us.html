About the Founders and Bakers

Evyy Café is the heartwarming creation of four passionate friends: Ellie, Viktoriya, Yasiel, and Yekaterina. Each brings a unique soul to the space—Yasiel, the dreamer with a flair for vintage aesthetics; Ellie, the storyteller whose warmth shapes every cozy corner; Yekaterina, a culinary artist inspired by her Eastern European roots; and Victoria, the strategist with a love for community and culture. Together, they've crafted more than just a retro café—they've created a sanctuary where nostalgia, flavor, and friendship meet.

Ellie

Co-founder & Head Baker

With a background in French pastry, Ellie brings precision and artistry to every creation. Her Devil's Food Cake has won local baking contests three years running. When not in the kitchen, she's exploring new flavor combinations inspired by her travels.

Viktoriya

Co-founder & Pastry Specialist

Viktoriya's Central Asian heritage infuses our menu with unique flavors and techniques. Her signature Napoleon Cake represents her philosophy of blending tradition with innovation. She's passionate about teaching others the joy of layered pastries.

Yasiel

Co-founder & Flavor Designer

Yasiel's Latin American roots shine through in our most vibrant desserts. His Tres Leches cake is legendary for its perfect balance of sweetness and texture. He's constantly experimenting with tropical fruits and spices to create new café favorites.

Yekaterina

Co-founder & Creative Director

With Moldovan heritage and an artist's eye, Yekaterina designs the visual elements of our desserts. Her Woodpile Cake showcases her talent for turning simple ingredients into edible masterpieces. She leads our cake decoration workshops on weekends.
