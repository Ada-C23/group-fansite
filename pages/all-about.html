Interesting Facts About Dik-Diks:

SCIENTIFIC NAME
MADOQUA KIRKII
WEIGHT
  • Average: 3-6 kg (6-13 lbs)
  • Newborns: ~0.7 kg (1.5 lbs)
  • Comparison: House cat-sized
SIZE
  • Shoulder height: 30-40.5 cm (12-16 in)
  • Body length: 52-67 cm (20-26 in)
  • Tail length: 3-5 cm (1-2 in)
LIFE SPAN
  • Wild: 4-5 years
  • Captivity: Up to 17 years
  • Threats: Predation limits wild lifespan
REGIONS
  • East Africa:
    • Somalia
    • Kenya
    • Ethiopia
    • Tanzania
  • Southern Africa:
    • Namibia
    • Angola
  • Preferred terrain: Arid/semi-arid zones with brush cover
HABITAT
  • Arid thorn scrub
  • Dense thickets
  • Open woodlands
  • Savannahs
  • Bushlands
  • Open plains
DIET
  • Leaves
  • Shoots
  • Fruits
  • Berries
  • Gets most water from food
PREDATORS
  • Eagles
  • Leopards
  • Jackals
  • Pythons
  • Humans (habitat destruction)
