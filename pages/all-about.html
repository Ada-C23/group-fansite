Interesting Facts About Dik-Diks:

SCIENTIFIC NAME
MADOQUA KIRKII
WEIGHT
3-6 kilograms (or 6-13 pounds)
SIZE
30-40.5 cm at the shoulder (12-16 inches) and 52-67 cm in length (20 to 26 inches)
LIFE SPAN
4-5 in the wild; Approximately up to 17 years in captivity
REGIONS
East Africa, from southern Somalia to central Tanzania and in Southern Africa from northern Namibia and parts of southwestern Angola
HABITAT
Ranges from arid, dense thorn scrub to thickets and open woodland, savannah, bushlands, and open plains
DIET
herbivorous
PREDATORS
humans, eagles, leopards, jackals, and pythons
