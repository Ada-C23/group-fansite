Natasha's friends testimonials

Janice Nicole Rosa Matos

Natasha's friend

Natasha is a friend, a guide, and a spiritual sister, she has blessed my life in ways I never knew I needed. She is a true embodiment of what it means to live freely, love deeply, and lead boldly. A proud first-gen Jamaican American, Natasha has faced life's challenges with grace, strength, and an unshakable commitment to justice and liberation. She speaks from the heart—always open and with purpose—carrying the voices of her ancestors and a deep connection to spirit. Her spirituality is sacred and grounding, and her passion for the liberation of all people oppressed by colonization is a fire that inspires everyone around her. Natasha's dedication to community healing has deep purpose in reconnecting people to their roots, their purpose, and their power. As a spiritual guide in our sangha, she devoted herself to the practice of Buddhism, leading with intention and grace. Her presence in these spaces was not only nurturing but transformative. She is a natural-born leader—decisive, intuitive, and fiercely loving. She lifts those around her and holds space in the most tender and powerful ways. She sees beauty in all things—people, nature, music, movement—and infuses her surroundings with vibrancy and magic. Her sense of music is unmatched; she curates the most perfect ambiance, whether it's a gathering of friends or a moment of stillness. Simply put, wherever Natasha goes, beauty follows.
