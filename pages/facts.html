Did You Know?

  • 🇬🇷 Greece has more than 6,000 islands, but only about 200 are inhabited.
  • 🏛️ The Olympic Games originated in ancient Greece around 776 BC.
  • 📜 Greek is one of the world’s oldest written languages still in use today.
  • ⛰️ Mount Olympus was believed to be the home of the Greek gods.
  • 🌊 Greece has over 13,000 kilometers of coastline—one of the longest in the world!
  • ⛰️ About 80% of Greece is mountainous—one of Europe’s most rugged countries.
  • 🏙️ Over 40% of the population lives in Athens, the capital.
  • 🫒 Greece is the third-largest producer of olives in the world.
  • 💙 White and blue are common colors in Greece, symbolizing sea, sky, and protection.
