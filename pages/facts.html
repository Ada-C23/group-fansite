Fun Facts 1: World’s Largest Pizza

The world’s largest pizza was made in Rome in 2012 and measured over 13,500 square feet! It was gluten-free and named “Ottavia” in honor of the first Roman emperor, Octavian Augustus.

Fun Facts 2:Americans Love Their Pizza

Each year, Americans eat about 3 billion pizzas, which averages to 46 slices per person! Pepperoni is the most popular topping.

Fun Facts 3: The First Pizzeria

The world’s first pizzeria, Antica Pizzeria Port'Alba, opened in Naples, Italy, in 1738. It’s still in business today!

Fun Fact 4: National Pizza Month

October is officially National Pizza Month in the United States. It was created in 1984 by a pizza industry publisher to celebrate one of America’s favorite foods!

Fun Fact 5: A Pizza in Space

In 2001, Pizza Hut became the first company to deliver pizza to space. A pizza was sent to the International Space Station for a Russian cosmonaut!

Fun Fact 6: The Most Expensive Pizza

The world’s most expensive pizza costs a whopping $12,000! The Louis XIII Pizza is topped with luxury ingredients like caviar, lobster, buffalo mozzarella, and 24-karat gold flakes.
