What Make Dogs So Special?

Here at the Dog Fansite, we never get tired of learning more about dogs, whether it’s a new study on canine behavior, a statistic from our database, or even just cool facts about a dog’s impressive physical and mental abilities. You may know some of these already, and others may be more of a “wait, really?” situation, but we think you’ll find all of them entertaining.

Let's check out some fun dog facts!🐾

  1. A dog’s nose print is unique, much like a person’s fingerprint.
  2. Speaking of sleeping … all dogs dream, but puppies and senior dogs dream more frequently than adult dogs.
  3. There are over 75 million pet dogs in the U.S. — more than in any other country.
  4. Dogs are not color-blind. They can see blue and yellow.
  5. All puppies are born deaf.
  6. Some studies suggest dogs are as intelligent as a two-year-old child.
