🍰 Fun Facts

🎉 The world's largest cake weighed over 25,000 pounds—baked in Alabama, USA!
🧠 The word “cake” comes from the Old Norse word 'kaka', dating back to Viking times.
🎂 Birthday cakes began in ancient Greece, where moon-shaped cakes were made for Artemis, goddess of the moon.

🤔 Did You Know?

The “naked cake” trend (cakes with minimal or no frosting on the sides) wasn't just for aesthetics—it was invented to reduce waste in high-end bakeries!
